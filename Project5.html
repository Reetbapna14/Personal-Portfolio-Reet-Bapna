Personal Branding

Dimension
23 cm X 32.5 cm
Medium
Adobe Illustrator
05

My logo is a true reflection of me, embodying my friendly and inclusive nature. The script's curves signify adaptability, and the overlapping initials represent my love for connections. Negative space in 'b' signifies my introverted-extroverted nature. The primary colors evoke calmness and warmth, complementing my brand's values and polished yet playful appearance. Building on this foundation, I extended the design philosophy to create brand stationery that not only embodies the brand's core values but also exudes a minimalist and professional aura. The stationary serves as an extension of the logo, creating a cohesive visual identity that communicates the essence of my brand.
