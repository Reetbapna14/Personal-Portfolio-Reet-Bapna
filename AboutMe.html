Hello, Welcome to Reet's Design Space


Hello there! I'm Reet Bapna, a passionate communication designer with a love for bringing ideas to life through the vibrant world of colors and fonts. As a communication designer, I thrive on the synergy between form and function, striving to weave compelling stories through visually striking designs.My canvas extends beyond pixels and paper, reaching into the realm of emotions and messages that resonate with the intended audience.


I'm a Communication Design sophomore at the Indian School of Design and Innovation, Mumbai, with a Professional Certificate in Communication Skills from Trinity College London. Currently a Coordinator at the Rotaract Club of Atlas Skilltech University, I've interned at Social Media By AK and Bloom Branding, gaining hands-on experience in marketing, design, and graphic design for diverse branding projects. Eager to combine academic knowledge with practical skills in communication design.


I am a fervent writer with a deep passion for the art of words. My love for writing extends beyond mere expression; it is a journey into the intricate realms of creativity and self-discovery. Poetry, in particular, holds a special place in my heart, both as a reader and a creator. The rhythmic dance of words and the power of metaphorical expression captivate my imagination.
